Workshop of CERN at IUT

This certification is from a three-day workshop at Isfahan University of Technology. In the workshop, we learned about bosons, the Higgs boson, and the CERN laboratory. We worked with data from CERN and tried to discover bosons in the same way scientists did when they found this particle.

My Bachelor Thesis

My thesis focuses on implementing the quantum teleportation protocol using IBM quantum computers. Throughout the thesis, I discuss quantum information theory and implement various quantum phenomena such as teleportation, superdense coding, and the Deutsch quantum algorithm. I conduct these implementations on both a local classical computer and an IBM quantum computer. Finally, I compare the results to analyze the functionality of each type of computer. Qiskit was the programming language I utilized for implementing these quantum phenomena in this project.

WorkShop of Quantum Sources

This certification program focuses on quantum sources, specifically delving into quantum single photon sources and detectors. Throughout the workshop, participants gained insights into various topics such as quantum mechanics, photonics, and related technologies. The program culminated in a visit to laboratories affiliated with Isfahan University, providing hands-on experience and practical exposure to the concepts discussed. Ultimately, this workshop served as a catalyst for me in selecting the topic for my Bachelor's thesis.
